# Exercise 1: Application Discovery

**Time:** 15 minutes  
**Objective:** Explore the Ticketyboo application and understand its functionality

## What You Will Learn
- How to explore a web application systematically
- How to identify key user journeys
- How to spot potential testing areas

## Instructions

### Step 1: Open the Application (2 minutes)
1. Open your web browser
2. Navigate to `http://localhost:3000`
3. Take a moment to observe the page layout

### Step 2: Explore the Main Features (8 minutes)
1. **Browse Events**
   - Look at the different events displayed
   - Note the information shown for each event (price, venue, date, tickets available)

2. **Test Event Filtering**
   - Click the "Concerts" button - what happens?
   - Click the "Films" button - what changes?
   - Click the "Comedy" button - notice the difference?
   - Click "All Events" - does everything return?

3. **Open an Event Details**
   - Click on any event
   - Examine the booking form that appears
   - Look at all the fields you need to fill in
   - **Do not complete a booking yet**
   - Close the modal by clicking the "×" button

### Step 3: Document Your Findings (5 minutes)
Write down your answers:
1. How many event categories are there?
2. What information do you need to book a ticket?
3. What payment information is required?
4. What questions would you ask as a tester?

## Expected Outcomes
- You understand the main features of Ticketyboo
- You can identify the booking process
- You have questions about how the system should behave

## Notes for Facilitators
- Encourage participants to explore freely
- Remind them not to complete actual bookings yet
- Help non-native speakers with any technical terms
